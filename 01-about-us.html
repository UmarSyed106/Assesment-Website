Who are we?

Destination Fiordland is run by a non-profit organization. We aim to encourage tourists and residents alike to experience the wonders of the south island, such as the Fiordlands. Our chairman, Cyrus McNeil, wishes to see the Fiordlands as a hotspot for tourism and adventuring.

A few words from McNeil

Hello traveler. I love the Fiordlands. I traveled to the fiordlands for over 30 years. I practically grew up here. I grew to feel a sense of connection towards the Fiordlands, almost like my secondary home. I want everyone to feel the same connection I feel towards the landscape. That is my ultimate goal and why Destination Fiordland exists.

What can I do at the Fiordlands?

People like you have participated in many activities here at the Fiordlands. Maybe you want to enjoy the in a Heli, or you want to splash down a river in a raft. The Fiordlands have it all! So what are you waiting for? Come on over and enjoy the landscape!
